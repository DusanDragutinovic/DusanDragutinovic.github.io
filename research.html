Research

My research lies in algebraic geometry and number theory, focusing on families of curves in positive characteristic from both theoretical and computational perspectives.

Keywords: curves, abelian varieties, characteristic p, families, supersingular, finite fields, Newton polygon, Ekedahl-Oort type, p-rank, a-number.
